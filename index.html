Muhammad Zuhair, also known as Green, is a 21-year-old young man of Moroccan origin. He is the founder of the Quincy team in 2020, specifically on December 12, the story behind naming the team quincy is his great admiration for the Quincy in his favorite anime, Bleach, who had awesome powers And a strong presence in the anime

Overview Of The Team's Goals :

The Quincy team's main goal is to create unique and an enjoyable content in Anime Power that will attract so many people wich will help developing Anime Power . We also aspire to develop the members joining the team in terms of design, drawing, and most other fields that may greatly benefit you in your daily and professional life. One of the most important goals of our team is to make new friends from different countries of the world, learn about cultures, the different customs and traditions of the Arab world to achieve Arab unification.
